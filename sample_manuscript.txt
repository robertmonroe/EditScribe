Chapter 1

The rain hammered against the windows of Detective Sarah Chen's office. She stared at the case file on her desk, the third unsolved murder in as many weeks. Each victim had been found with the same cryptic symbol carved into their palm.

"You're still here?" Marcus appeared in the doorway, his tie loosened after a long day.

Sarah looked up. "Someone has to figure this out."

"It's past midnight. Go home."

She shook her head. "Not until I find the connection."

Marcus sighed and pulled up a chair. "Then I'm staying too."
