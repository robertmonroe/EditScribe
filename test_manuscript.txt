Chapter 1

The rain hammered against Detective Sarah Chen's office windows. Three unsolved murders in three weeks.

"Still here?" Marcus asked from the doorway.

Sarah nodded. "Someone has to solve this."

Chapter 2

The fourth victim lay in an alley. Same symbol carved into the palm. But this time, a clue - a paper with an address.

"1247 Riverside Drive," Marcus read.

"Let's go," Sarah said.
